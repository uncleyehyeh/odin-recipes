Vegetable Risotto Recipe

How To Make Vegetable Risotto

The sweetness of the ingredients gives this risotto a mild flavor.

Ingredients
  • Any Carrot or Any Pumpkin
  • Hylian Rice
  • Goat Butter
  • Rock Salt
Steps
  1. First press the + button to enter your menu.
  2. From there, scroll over to the ingredients tab
  3. Click on a Carrot and select "Hold."
  4. Click on a Hylian Rice.
  5. Click on a Goat Butter.
  6. Click on a Rock Salt.
  7. Once you have all the ingredients you want to cook, exit the menu and walk over to the open pot. When you get close enough, there will be a prompt telling you to Cook your ingredients.
  8. Toss your ingredients into the pot and after a few seconds a cooked meal will be done.
