Skooma Recipe

How To Make Skooma

Because skooma is so highly sought-after,yet so illegal, some enterprising innkeepers across Tamriel have cooked up their own version of a cordial that mimics that addictively sweet drink so loved by the Khajit.

Ingredients
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup boiling water
  • 2 cups vodka
  • 10 pitted dried apricots, diced
  • Pinch of ground cardamom
  • Dash of vanilla extract
Steps
  1. Combine the sugar and boiling water in a small heat-proof container, and stir until sugar has dissolved.
  2. Pour into a clean bottle, then add the remaining.
  3. Cap the bottle and sit somewhere dim to infuse, shaking occasionally.
  4. Allow to steep for 2 to 3 weeks, or up to 1 month for maximum flavor.
  5. Strain out the apricots and store the Skooma in a glass bottle.
  6. Serve in small cordial glasses.
